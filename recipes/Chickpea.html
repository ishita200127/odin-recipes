Mediterranean Chickpea Salad

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas
  • cucumber
  • tomatoes
  • red onion (optional)
  • olives
  • parsley
  • lemon juice
  • olive oil
  • salt
  • pepper

Method

  1. Mix chopped veggies + chickpeas.
  2. Add lemon juice, olive oil, salt, pepper.
  3. Toss well and serve fresh.
