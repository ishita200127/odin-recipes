Chinese Veg Stir-Fry Noodles

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Broccoli
  • Carrots
  • Bell peppers
  • Soy sauce
  • Vinegar
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Salt

Method

  1. Boil the noodles and set aside.
  2. Stir-fry garlic and vegetables on high heat.
  3. Add soy sauce, vinegar, sesame oil, and salt.
  4. Add noodles and toss well for 2 minutes.
