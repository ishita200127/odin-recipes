Greek Veg Pita Wrap

Ingredients

  • Pita bread
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Olives
  • Hummus
  • Feta cheese (optional)
  • Lemon juice

Method

  1. Warm the pita bread.
  2. Spread hummus inside the pita.
  3. Add chopped vegetables and olives.
  4. Drizzle lemon juice, fold, and serve.
