French Ratatouille

Ingredients

  • Eggplant
  • Zucchini
  • Tomatoes
  • Onions
  • Bell peppers
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Thyme or basil
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Method

  1. Sauté onions and garlic in olive oil.
  2. Add all chopped vegetables.
  3. Season with herbs, salt, and pepper.
  4. Cover and simmer for 20–25 minutes.
  5. Serve warm with bread or rice.
